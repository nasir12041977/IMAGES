1. ADAL The Most Just & Fair
Analysis & Data Archive Logic (The Brain & Lifetime Data Library)
◈ Real-Time Fleet Control It manages and controls more than 50 software sets in real-time.
◈ Dynamic Coin Management It automatically adds new coins listed on the exchange and removes delisted ones.
◈ Lifetime Data History It archives every coin's complete history from day one for an indefinite period.
◈ Multi-Timeframe Performance Ranking It tracks data across multiple timeframes and assigns performance ranks based on back-testing results.
◈ Pure Data-Driven Execution The software does not rely on news, charts, candles, or predefined strategies.
◈ Adaptive Data Analysis It builds its own strategy by analyzing raw incoming data in real-time to decide its next moves.

2. QADR The Power & Value
Quality Check & Deployment Rating (The Supervisor & Signal Validator)
◈ Cloud Infrastructure Monitoring It acts as a supervisor, monitoring the health of all software sets running on the cloud to prevent technical failures.
◈ Signal Strength & Rating It evaluates the status of specific coins across all 50 software sets to determine the actual strength and quality of a signal.
◈ Detailed Activity Tracking It highlights every single movement of a coin within each software, providing a granular view of its behavior.

3. DASTAK The Knock & Access
Data Analysis & Strategy Tracking (The Master Strategist & Netting Core)
◈ Signal Aggregation It collects all incoming signals from every software to decide which coin is suitable for trading.
◈ Strategic Position Management It determines the side (BuySell) and the specific coins that should be traded or ignored.
◈ Quantity Optimization It calculates the exact volume and quantity required for each position.
◈ The Power of Netting It resolves contradictions between different software signals (e.g., if one says Buy and another says Sell) using its advanced netting logic.
◈ Final Decision Core By canceling out opposing data through networking and netting, it identifies the true market direction and moves forward with the net quantity.

4. AMAL The Action & Practice
Asset Matching & Arbitration Logic (The Auditor & Gap Finder)
◈ Position Reconciliation It compares all open positions on the exchange with the required positions generated by the software.
◈ Trade Execution Decision It decides which specific positions need to be closed and where new entries must be taken.
◈ Discrepancy Detection It identifies any differences or gaps between the software’s data and the actual exchange positions.
◈ Seamless Alignment It completely eliminates the variance between the software and the exchange, ensuring both are in perfect sync.

5. SAFEER The Ambassador & Messenger
Secure Audit & Final Execution Report (The Final Executor & API Sync)
◈ Precision Execution It ensures that all exchange positions match the exact direction and quantity decided by the software—not a single unit more or less.
◈ Gap Elimination It completely bridges any remaining gap between the software's logic and the exchange's reality.
◈ API Integration It utilizes API keys to securely and instantly execute commands on the exchange.
◈ Instant Synchronization In the blink of an eye, it eliminates all discrepancies, aligning every position with the software’s final decision.
